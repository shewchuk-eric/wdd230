Princeville Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Purpose:

The Princeville Chamber of Commerce works to serve the businesses, homeowners and visitors who make our community on the Hawaiian island of Kauai a healthy and vibrant place to be. We work to promote and sustain the local businesses that provide valuable products and services. As leaders of our community, our members also work to organize, develop, and support various local activities like Family Movie Night, held during summer months (check back for schedule in late May).

Scenarios:

  • How do I join?
  • Can I volunteer?
  • Are the Farmers Markets part of the chamber?
  • How many businesses and individuals are members?
  • Are there regular meetings? What's the schedule?
  • How many people does the chamber serve?
